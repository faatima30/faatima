Nadiifiye web app

Nadiifiye was developed as part of a hackathon project, focused on organizing and managing cleaning campaigns in public places such as beaches. The platform facilitates volunteer coordination, allowing users to join, create, and manage cleaning initiatives aimed at promoting cleaner and healthier environments. My primary responsibility was leading the frontend development, ensuring a user-friendly interface for volunteers and campaign organizers. I also contributed to some backend functionalities, ensuring smooth integration between the frontend and the database.
